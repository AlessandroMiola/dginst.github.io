The Digital Gold Institute is a research and development center, focused on teaching, consulting, and advising about scarcity in digital domain (bitcoin and crypto-assets) and the underlying blockchain technology.

We explore the foundations and can assist you in understanding them: cryptography, game theory, networking and data transmission, economic and monetary theory. We love to get our hands dirty in the programming and technology fields, engaging in a productive journey with our partners.
