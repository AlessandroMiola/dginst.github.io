The Digital Gold Institute is a research and development center about scarcity in the digital domain (bitcoin and crypto-assets) and blockchain technology.

Focused on its fellowship program, the Institute promotes the digital scarcity experiment in the public debate and the scientific academia through R&D, teaching, consulting, and advising.

We explore the foundations and can assist in understanding them: cryptography, monetary theory, computer networking, game theory. Of course, we write code and love to get our hands dirty in programming and technology.

The times they are a-changin'... follows us on social networks and, even better, get in touch: we are engaged in a productive journey with our partners.
